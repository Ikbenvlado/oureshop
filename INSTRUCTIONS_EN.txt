AS A CUSTOMER
=============

1. BROWSING PRODUCTS
   - The home page displays all available products
   - Filter by category (Electronics, Clothing, Furniture...)
   - Sort by price, rating or name
   - Search products by name
   - Switch language SK / EN (button in the top right corner)

2. PRODUCT DETAIL
   - Click on any product to open its detail page
   - Product photo with zoom option (click the image)
   - Description, rating, availability, discount
   - "Add to cart" button
   - Related products from the same category
   - Customer reviews (approved only)
   - Form to write your own review (requires login)

3. CART
   - Added products appear in the mini cart (icon in the top right)
   - The /cart page shows the full cart
   - Change quantity or remove products
   - Shows subtotal, shipping cost and total
   - Free shipping above a certain order value

4. CHECKOUT
   - Step 1: Enter your name, email, phone and delivery address
   - Optionally apply a discount coupon code
   - Step 2: Pay by card via Stripe
   - Test card: 4242 4242 4242 4242 | exp: 12/28 | CVV: 123
   - After payment you receive a confirmation email

5. CUSTOMER ACCOUNT (/account)
   - Registration and login
   - Edit profile (name, email, phone)
   - Order history
   - Saved delivery addresses
   - Favorite products (heart icon on product cards)
   - Change password
   - Forgot password → reset via email

6. REVIEWS
   - A logged-in customer can write a review for any product
   - Star rating (1–5) + text comment
   - The review is only shown after admin approval
   - Customer receives an email about approval or rejection

7. CONTACT
   - Page /contact — contact form
   - After submission, an email is sent to the owner and an auto-reply to the customer

8. INFORMATION PAGES
   - /o-nas                        — About Us
   - /obchodne-podmienky           — Terms & Conditions
   - /ochrana-osobnych-udajov      — Privacy Policy (GDPR)
   - /reklamacie                   — Returns & Complaints

AS AN ADMINISTRATOR
===================

Login: https://ourstone.fun/admin
Login credentials: provided separately

DASHBOARD (/admin/dashboard)
   - Total revenue, number of orders, customers and products
   - Revenue chart for the last 30 and 7 days
   - Order count chart
   - Top 5 products by revenue

PRODUCTS (/admin/products)
   - Add, edit and delete products
   - Set price, discount, stock, category, image URL
   - English name and description (for the EN version of the site)
   - Product variants (e.g. Size: S, M, L, XL)
   - CSV export of products
   - Bulk delete multiple products at once

ORDERS (/admin/orders)
   - Overview of all orders
   - 7 statuses: new → processing → shipped → delivered / cancelled / returned / awaiting payment
   - Internal notes on each order
   - Manual order creation
   - Print HTML invoice

CUSTOMERS (/admin/customers)
   - List of all customers
   - Sort by name, order count, total spent, registration date
   - Filter: active / blocked
   - Block a customer (blocked users cannot log in)
   - Customer detail with full order history

REVIEWS (/admin/reviews)
   - List of all customer reviews
   - Filter: pending / approved / rejected
   - Badge showing the number of reviews awaiting approval
   - Approve → review appears on the site, customer receives an email
   - Reject → review is not shown, customer receives an email

COUPONS (/admin/coupons)
   - Create discount codes
   - Types: percentage discount / fixed € discount / free shipping
   - Settings: minimum order value, maximum uses, expiry date
   - Activate / deactivate a coupon
   - Counter showing how many times a coupon has been used

PAYMENTS (/admin/payments)
   - Overview of the last 50 Stripe payments
   - Filter by payment status
   - Stripe account balance (available + pending)

ADMINISTRATORS (/admin/admins)
   - List of all admin accounts
   - Create a new admin account with a role:
     · editor      — product management
     · support     — orders, customers, reviews
     · admin       — full access
     · super_admin — full access + admin management

SETTINGS (/admin/settings)
   - Admin profile (change name and password)
   - Store information (name, company ID, address, VAT %)
   - Email settings (sender name, email subjects, footer)

EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS (automatic)
=================================

   Customer receives an email when:
   ✓ They complete an order (order confirmation)
   ✓ They request a password reset (reset link)
   ✓ They submit a message via the contact form (auto-reply)
   ✓ Their review has been approved or rejected

   Admin receives an email when:
   ✓ A customer places a new order
   ✓ A customer submits a message via the contact form
   ✓ A customer writes a new review (awaiting approval)
